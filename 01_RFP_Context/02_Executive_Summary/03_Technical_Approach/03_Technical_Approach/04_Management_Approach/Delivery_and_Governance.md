## Delivery and Governance Model

### Delivery Framework
Project ORION will be executed using an iterative, milestone-based delivery model that balances agility with governance.

### Governance Structure

- Weekly status reviews

### Risk Management
Risks are identified early, documented, and actively managed through mitigation plans and stakeholder engagement, ensuring predictable and transparent delivery.
